# About this site

I would like to create my own site in the Internet that is entirely me. No algorithm. No ads. A site that does not trace your privacy, does not collect your data. 

Those are the problems for social networking sites nowadays. 

Those sites are controlled by only a few big tech, e.g. Facebook, Whatsapp and Instagram are all controlled by [Facebook, Inc](https://about.facebook.com/company-info/). Those big tech can ban any users they do not like; they can change their algorithms to control the public opinions. That is unfair as those giant enterprises have unparalleled power and they are not politically neutral. We are forced to accept their ideologies.

Moreover, those media are controlled by "influencers". They create contents according to trends and algorithms. And their contents dominate our feeds. I want to see how my friends going but not those strangers.

That's why I create my personal site.

I had tried to use "Jekyll", the default site builder for GitHub page, to build this site before, but I found that it was not good enough. I have done some research and eventually chosen site generator "[Docusaurus](https://docusaurus.io/)". I think it is one of the best site generators at present.
