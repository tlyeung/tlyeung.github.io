About this site

I would like to create my own site in the Internet that is entirely me. No algorithm. No ads. A site that does not trace your privacy.

There are many problems for social network nowadays.

First, they are controlled by only a few big tech, e.g. Facebook, Whatsapp and Instagram are all controlled by Facebook. They can ban any users they do not like; they can change their algorithm to control the public opinion. That is unfair as those big tech have unparalleled power and they are not politically neutral. We are forced to accept their ideologies.

Moreover, those media are controlled by "influencers". They create contents according to trend and algorithm. Their contents dominate our feeds. I want to see how my friends going but not those strangers. There are just too many rubbish in Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.

That's why I create my personal site.

I had tried to use "Jekyll", the default site builder for GitHub page, to build this site before, but I found that it was not good enough. I have done some research and eventually chosen site generator "Docusaurus". I think it is the best site generator at present.
