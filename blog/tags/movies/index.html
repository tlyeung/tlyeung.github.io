Movies June 2021

June 5, 2021 · One min read

I watched a movie "Persian lessons" on 5th June. It is a story about a jew pretend to be a Persian, to try to survive in a concentration camp during WWII. The plot was interesting but there were also some flaws. But, all in all, it is still a good movie to watch. The two main characters were outstanding. I give this movie a 7.5/10.

Movies May 2021

May 28, 2021 · 2 min read

I watched a movie "Promising Young Woman" on 4th May. It was the 2021 Oscar winner of the best original screenplay. But I don't think it deserved such honour. It seems average to me. Maybe there were fewer movies last year due to COVID. Some screens reminded me of another movie this year, "I care a lot", and I found them very unrealistic that a woman could scare off angry men that easily. According to my experience, embarrassed men tend to use violence even more. My girlfriend did not like the revenge idea, I however disagreed with her. Revenge is a must when it is needed. Revenge is one of the common themes in many good movies, even among those all time greats such as the Godfather. I give this film a 6/10.

I watched (but did not finish) the movie "Mank" on Netflix on 28th May. It was disappointing. I do not oppose black and white movies, some of them are my favourites, e.g. Casablanca. But the production of "Mank" was, I don't know how to say it better, bad. It looks like a youtube video, or the work by a freshman in movie college. If there is a black and white movie, I expect the cinematography should be like "Schindler's List", or "The Artist". "Mank" was nowhere near that standard. I have never watched a worse movie by David Fincher than this.
