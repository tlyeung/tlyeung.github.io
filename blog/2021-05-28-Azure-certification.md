tags: [Programming]

I just certified in "Azure Developer Associate (AZ-204)" yesterday, despite I only have limited experience in Azure before. I studied a online course earlier this month. But I have to admit I am not very good at it.

I don't know what should be the standard but the test was simply too easy to pass; I did not even study much for it. In the exam, when I found out that I did not know what the correct answer was, I guess. Or more accurately, I used deduction and chose the one I thought was the correct answer.

The certification has a relatively short expiration period. I did not pay for the exam, and I probably will never pay for this exam in the future.
