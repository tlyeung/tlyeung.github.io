I watched a movie "[Persian lessons](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt9738784/)" on 5th June. It is a story about a jew pretend to be a Persian, to try to survive in a concentration camp during WWII. The plot was interesting but there were also some flaws. But, all in all, it is still a good movie to watch. The two main characters were outstanding. I give this movie a 7.5/10.

I watched a movie "[Quo Vadis, Aida?](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quo_Vadis,_Aida%3F)" on 26th June. This was a powerful movie. I have heard of what had happened in Yugoslavia in 1990s, but it was my first time to see the massacre story in cinema. History repeats itself. I felt sorrow for the Bosnians but I felt more furious to the UN. It should take much of the responsibility of the tragedy because of their indifference and inability. The UN peacekeeping force were soft, weak, and useless, and so stupid to make wrong decisions after wrong decisions. The UN should be closed, it is a completely waste of money. What is the point of its existence if they could not save the people who had already sheltered in their base, and let them to be killed just next to their base? I think this is the same for all international organizations that involves so many nations, such as WHO, which should take the responsibility of the COVID outbreak for its slow reaction. When I watched the movie, I was thinking, what if there was a journalist in the crowd, or if the UN force can contact a journalist to bring this to the attention of the people from all around the world, people would know and maybe thousands of lives can be saved. That thought reminds me of Apple Daily, I feel much more unsecured to live in Hong Kong after [its closure](https://hongkongfp.com/2021/06/23/breaking-last-edition-of-apple-daily-to-print-no-later-than-sat-as-board-forced-to-halt-all-hong-kong-operations/). I will rate this movie 8.5/10.


I have watch movie “[Cruella](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3228774/)” on 29th June. It was a high quality Disney movie, which exceeded my expectation. The story was quite fresh, Emma Stone and Emma Thomson made it even better. The character Jasper was however vague, definitely not a lover, not quite a friend, not quite a follower, of Cruella/ Stella, his story line was wasted. I will rate the movie 8/10.
