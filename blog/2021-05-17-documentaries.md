I watched a movie "[Promising Young Woman](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt9620292/)" on 4th May. It is the Oscar winner of Best Original Screenplay. I do not think it is that good. Maybe there were only a few movies last year.  Some screens remind me of another movie this year, "[I care a lot](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt09893250/)", and I find them very unrealistic that a woman can scare off angry men that easily. According to my experience, embarrassed men tend to use violence even more. My girlfriend do not like the revenge idea, I however disagree with her. Revenge is a must when it is needed, and it makes audiences feel good. Revenge is one of the common themes in many good movies, even among those all time greats, such as the Godfather. I give it a 6/10.

>"Life finds a way" 

I watched a French documentary, called "[The Immortals](https://harbour-rights.com/detail.php?id=7888)", on RTHK TV on 13th May. It is about some of the animals that have powerful recover ability, or simply do not age, or can survive in the most extreme environment. Do you know lobster, a common seafood, is one of the animals that never age? Human is probably the biggest threat to those old giant lobsters, and so to many other animals as well.

I watched a French documentary on RTHK TV on 17th May, it is called "[Le génie des arbres](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt12323578/)". The French title means "The tree genius". Of course it is about trees. Trees are amazing creatures, they have senses like us. We know the fruits from the trees, we know how to make rubber or oil from trees, they are much more sophisticated than those. Trees do not have brains and do not move much, but in certain ways, they are quite similar to animals. Even scientists are still just starting to understand them.
